Referral Commission Payout Policy

Official Policy on Eligibility, Tiers, Payment, and Exclusions.

IMPORTANT NOTE ON PAYMENT

As required by the Income Tax Act, Cyber Alliance India will deduct TDS (Tax Deducted at Source) from all commission payments exceeding the statutory threshold. All commission figures below are the Gross Commission before TDS.

This commission is a commitment from our brand to reward your support and is not covered by inflating the client's project price.

2. Payment and General Conditions

Item Detail
Payment Method Account Transfer (NEFT/IMPS) after necessary statutory deductions.
Completion Clause Entire commission is paid within 15 days of Cyber Alliance India receiving the full payment for the project from the client.
Project Cancellation If a project is canceled, the commission will be calculated on the established percentage of the non-refundable project advance received from the client, with payment to be made within 15 days of that advance being secured.
Requirement Referrers must provide all necessary account details and a valid PAN card/Tax ID within 60 days of the client's final payment for the referred project. Failure to provide these details within this timeframe will result in the forfeiture of the commission.

3. Referral Validity and Exclusions

Commission is applicable only for referrals that lead to projects in Website Development and Web Application Development.

Exclusion Type Policy Detail
Existing Clients Commission is paid solely for new client acquisitions. If a referred new client has multiple simultaneous projects, commission is awarded exclusively for one initial project, as designated by Cyber Alliance India. All other current and subsequent projects with that client are ineligible.
Self-Referral Individuals are ineligible to receive commission for referring their own company, a company they own/partner in, or a project where they are the primary direct contact/decision-maker.

4. Legal and Administrative

Independent Contractor Status

The referrer acts solely as an independent contractor. This policy does not establish an employment relationship, partnership, or joint venture. Referrers have no authority to bind Cyber Alliance India to any contract.

Tax Deduction and Responsibility (TDS)

  • Cyber Alliance India is mandated to deduct Tax Deducted at Source (TDS) from the gross commission payment, as per the relevant sections of the Indian Income Tax Act, 1961 (e.g., Section 194H or 194J), if the payment exceeds the statutory threshold.
  • The payment made will be the gross commission less the applicable TDS.
  • The referrer is solely responsible for their final income tax, GST, and other local, state, and federal taxes on the gross commission income.
  • A valid PAN card/Tax ID must be provided for payment processing and for the company to issue the necessary TDS certificate (Form 16A) for the referrer to claim credit.

Right to Modify and Governing Law

Cyber Alliance India reserves the right to review, amend, or terminate this policy, including rates and criteria, at any time without prior notice. Commission eligibility is determined by the policy in effect on the date the client's project advance is successfully received. This policy shall be governed by the laws of India.
