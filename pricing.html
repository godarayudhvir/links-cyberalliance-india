Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the "Basic Support"?

Basic support covers bug fixes related to our code, server uptime monitoring, and minor text/image updates for 30 days after launch. It does not include new feature development.

Do I own the code after the project is done?

Yes. Once the final payment is settled, you own 100% of the source code and design assets. We do not lock you into proprietary platforms.

Can I upgrade my Shopify plan later?

Absolutely. Shopify is built to scale. We can help you transition from the Basic Setup to a Professional setup as your product catalog grows.
